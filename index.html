Thank you for buying the PRO version of Hydejack! 🎉

More than one year ago I was looking for a blogging platform but couldn’t find it: One that would let me write posts in markdown with my own editor, one that didn’t look awful buy modern design standards, and one that felt snappy on a mobile phone. That and more is now Hydejack. I hope you enjoy it as much as I do.
